Welcome!

I’m a queer/trans therapist who is passionate about creating collaborative therapeutic relationships that center consent and feedback from you.

I have a mutual aid background related to prison abolition, sex work, disability, and trans healthcare. My approach is influenced by antiracism, anticapitalism, fat activist, queer/trans affirming, and disability centered lenses. I believe that healing happens in relationship/community and encourage healthy conflict.

My therapeutic style is compatible with individuals and relational units looking to deepen their sense of self, create and maintain boundaries, and set short and longer-term goals. I’m interested in working with clients that value an abolitionist and anti-oppressive lens being brought into their healing work. I love working with individuals, couples, families, small groups, polycules, siblings, friends, ect.

I offer sliding scale rates based on need/marginalized identity. I'm open to weekly, biweekly, or monthly appointment frequency and happy to adjust that up and down over time. I’m covid-cautious and see clients through telehealth.
