I'm an Marriage and Family associate therapist who works from a from an antiracist, anticapitalist, fat-affirming, disability-centered, queer/trans-affirming lens.  I have a mutual aid background related to prison abolition, sex work, disability, and trans healthcare. 

I want to create a collaborative therapeutic relationship that meets you where you’re at. I have an informal/friendly vibe with clients and believe that it takes time to build trust in therapy. It’s important to me that our work together be consent-focused, non-judgmental, and include feedback on what is and isn’t working for you. 

I'm compatible with individuals/relationships looking to deepen their sense of self, create and maintain boundaries, and set short and longer-term goals. I’m interested in working with clients that value an abolitionist and anti-oppressive lens being brought into their healing work. I believe that healing happens in relationships/community and encourage healthy conflict.

I am covid-cautious and see clients through telehealth.
